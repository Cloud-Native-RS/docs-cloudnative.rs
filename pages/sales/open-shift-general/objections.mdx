# Objection Handling

## Common Objections & Responses  

- **“We already use Kubernetes, why OpenShift?”**  
  → OpenShift provides enterprise-grade automation, security, and lifecycle management that reduce operational risk and cost.  

- **“It’s too expensive compared to DIY Kubernetes.”**  
  → DIY has hidden costs in integration, support, and downtime. OpenShift accelerates adoption and lowers TCO over time.  

- **“We don’t want to be locked in.”**  
  → OpenShift is based on open-source Kubernetes, fully portable across clouds and on-prem.  

- **“We already have VMware.”**  
  → OpenShift enables cost optimization and modernization beyond VMware, with proven migration paths and reduced licensing costs.
